<objective>
Exclude perfmon events from every ranking stage at exactly one seam, and prove twice over that
nothing else moved: that a case's cluster output is byte-identical with and without a perfmon CSV,
and that every perfmon sample remains individually retrievable for citation.

Purpose: PERF-03 and roadmap criteria 4 and 5. This is the only plan in the phase that edits shipped
v1.0/v1.1 pipeline behaviour, and the cross-cutting regression risk recorded in STATE.md § Blockers
is concentrated here. It runs last, after the adapter is green, by design.
Output: the `store.py` exclusion plus its regression and citation guards.
</objective>

    should be a visible change at that moment.

    immediately below `iter_event_summaries`, shares its ordering expression and its streaming idiom,

    oversight and invites a tidy-up into a shared helper. Add an explanatory comment at the seam, on
    both methods, stating that the difference is deliberate, naming PERF-03 and the citation
    requirement, and pointing at the paired tests below. Modify nothing about `iter_event_rows`'s
