<objective>
Port the reference `parse_log` attribution (`docs/reference/analyze_dss8.py:293-394`)
onto the Phase-9 typed models as THREE independent dimensions — by OID, by
`Source=` request type, and by SID (session) — resolving the one-OID/many-SID
fan-out that the flat reference nested under OID (D-14). Every attributed figure
carries the owning grant-line `event_id`s (D-16), the `cited ⊆ store` bridge Phase
11 reuses verbatim. Then land the single orchestration entry point `analyse_mcm`
that composes `select_window` (Plan 01) + `compute_flags` (Plan 02) +
`attribute_window` into the `McmAnalysis` the report/CLI consume — the completion
of MCM-04.

Purpose: attribution is what turns "the server denied memory" into "which
sessions/objects/request-types consumed the lead-up headroom"; the window
boundary (lead-up only, never post-denial) and the event_id provenance are the two
correctness invariants.
Output: `AttributionRow`/`Attribution`/`EpisodeAnalysis`/`McmAnalysis` +
`attribute_window` + `analyse_mcm`.
</objective>
