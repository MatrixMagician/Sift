<objective>
Build the deterministic MCM fact renderer and its versioned prompt fragment: a pure
function `render_mcm_facts(analysis: McmAnalysis) -> tuple[str, set[str]]` plus
`src/sift/prompts/mcm_facts.md`. The renderer fills the fragment's placeholders from
`analyse_mcm` output — episode summary, denial-time breakdown as % of HWM/total,
graded flags, and top-5 attributions per dimension (D-19) — emitting `[evt:<id>]`
lines and returning the citable ids. This is the model-free, byte-identical-on-re-run
source of truth that the Wave 2 splice consumes.

Purpose: MCM-06 requires MCM figures that are computed, never authored by the model.
This plan isolates the computation-to-text step so the numbers provably originate in
Python (D-20) and the fragment file carries no values.
Output: `mcm_facts.md` fragment, `pipeline/mcm_facts.py::render_mcm_facts`, unit tests.
</objective>
