<objective>
Land the two window-input fields Phase 9 deliberately deferred (`hwm_bytes`,
`avail_timeline`) on `McmEpisode`, populate them in `detect_episodes`, and add the
non-interactive lead-up-window selector `select_window` ŌĆö the auto-window half of
MCM-04 (D-13). This is a faithful port of the reference `prompt_window`
(`docs/reference/analyze_dss8.py:678-773`) minus the `input()` prompt: only the
widest 25%-of-HWM descent threshold (the reference's Enter-default `options[0]`).

Purpose: everything downstream (attribution in Plan 03, the report window header
in Plan 04) needs the auto-selected window span; the window is the lead-up
`[start ŌĆ” denial)`, never the whole episode.
Output: `EpisodeWindow` model + `select_window()` + the populated episode fields,
plus a new pre-denial multi-SID descending-`AvailableMCM` fixture that both this
plan's descent test and Plan 03's SID fan-out consume.
</objective>
